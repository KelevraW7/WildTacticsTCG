Link to current documentation: https://octanta-studio.gitbook.io/touch-effect-system-for-unity/

For quick setup:

1. Import the asset into your Unity project.
2. Open Assets > OctantaStudio > TouchEffectSystem > Prefabs > TouchEffects.
3. Add any of the effect prefabs to your scene canvas, for example the TouchCircle prefab, and unpack it.
4. Optional: In the prefab component, assign the Target Canvas as the current canvas, or it will be assigned automatically at runtime.
5. Run the scene to test. Also use DemoScene to explore all available prefabs.

---

Tip: In the TouchGlowUI component fing Glow Material reference, and adjust the effect before running.
